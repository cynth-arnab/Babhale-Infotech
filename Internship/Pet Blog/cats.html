The Mysterious Domestic Creature

Cats are like tiny, unpredictable software programs with chaotic behaviors and occasional crashes. One moment, they purr in perfect harmony; the next, they launch themselves off furniture—like a process stuck in an infinite loop.

They follow unconventional debugging methods, casually knocking things off tables just to test gravity. Their security model? A strict zero-trust approach—analyzing your every move before granting access to belly rubs (which might be a honeypot).

Essentially, a cat is an open-source cryptid—available for study, but never fully understood.
