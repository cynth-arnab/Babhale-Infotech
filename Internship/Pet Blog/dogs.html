The Friendly Domestic Companion

Dogs are like well-optimized, user-friendly programs—reliable, predictable, and always eager to execute commands. Their default mode is loyalty.exe, running in an infinite loop with a subroutine for tail-wagging and face-licking.

Unlike cats, dogs operate on a full-trust model—no authentication required. Walk in the door? Immediate access granted with maximum enthusiasm. They also double as excellent error handlers—drop food on the floor, and they’ll promptly clean up the mess, no debugging needed.

If cats are UNIX systems (powerful but requiring expertise), dogs are like a well-designed GUI—intuitive, friendly, and always eager to help. Their emotions are clear, their intentions transparent, and their enthusiasm permanently set to **100%**.
